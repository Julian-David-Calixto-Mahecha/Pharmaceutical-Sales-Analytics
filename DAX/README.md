# DAX

## Overview

DAX was used to create business metrics and KPI calculations that support sales performance monitoring, budget compliance analysis, and product portfolio evaluation.

The measures were designed to provide meaningful business insights while maintaining calculation efficiency within the Power BI model.

---

## Main KPIs

### YTD Sales

Calculates total sales accumulated during the selected period.

### YTD Budget

Calculates total budget accumulated during the selected period.

### Compliance Rate

Measures the percentage of budget achieved.

### Freshness Index

Evaluates the proportion of recently launched products within the portfolio.

### Title modeling

Creates a dynamic KPI title that automatically displays a visual status indicator based on the compliance rate value.

---

## Business Value

These measures allow users to:

- Monitor sales performance.
- Compare actual results against budget targets.
- Evaluate portfolio freshness.
- Support data-driven decision making.
